Musical taste study for Transylvania University students
Erik Mudrak 2016

Python scripts: (.py)
	users - prompts and prints results for each individual user
	transy - prompts and prints results for all users, overall
	*WIP* fields - prompts and prints results depending on supplied fields (e.g. sophomores, students from KY, Delta Sigs)
	*WIP* time - prompts and prints results based on timestamp of song, manipulating data to look at time of day and week

Other files are csv and text files for input/output
